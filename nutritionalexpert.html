Meet Our Expert

Welcome to our Health and Wellness community! Our expert, Dr. Megan Coltrin, is a certified nutritionist with over 5 years of experience in holistic wellness. Dr. Coltrin believes in empowering individuals to make sustainable, healthy choices.

After experiencing my own health issues, I decided to take matters into my own hands and learn how to become the healthiest version of myself. I also provide corporate chats for companies looking to support their employee's performance, health, and overall happiness."
